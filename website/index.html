Everything a fiction writer needs

From first draft to final manuscript, Cahnon keeps your story organized and consistent.

Scene Organization

Organize scenes into chapters with drag-and-drop reordering, status tracking, and word count.

Story Bible

Centralize characters, locations, objects, factions, concepts, and glossary terms in one place.

Timeline & Conflicts

Track events across your story and detect timeline inconsistencies automatically.

Plot Arcs

Define narrative arcs, link them to scenes and characters, and track their progression visually.

Corkboard & Annotations

Visualize your story structure on a corkboard. Add inline annotations for revision notes.

Local & Private

Everything stored in a single .cahnon file on your machine. No cloud, no accounts, no tracking.

How it works

Start writing immediately. Add structure when you need it.

01

Create a project

Open Cahnon and start a new project. A single .cahnon file holds everything.

02

Write your scenes

Focus on your story. Organize into chapters, add bible entries, and track arcs as you go.

03

Revise & export

Use annotations and conflict detection to polish. Export as Markdown, PDF, DOCX, or JSON.
